House plants are a great way to provide clean air in your home, as they naturally filter the air! They also offer a comforting feel to any space and can bring all sorts of color and design. There are hundreds of kinds of plants but can also be divided into two types of plants: leafy and succulents. It can be overwehlming at first but once you get the hang of it, it can be addicting! Many people treat their house plants as their children or babies and cherish them so!
